XuanFu: The woman Xuanfu nevus is a kind of Hainanese dialect, which means that a woman exposes her husband and wife mole. Husband and wife mole refers to the moles on the same body of both husband and wife, and legend has the meaning of mutual attraction and fate life. On Hainan Island, it is a ritual and tradition for women to declare to the society that they have married a woman and share a couple's mole with their husband. In modern society, the tradition of women having sex has gradually declined, especially in cities.
